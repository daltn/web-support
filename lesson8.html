Custom Domain with Namecheap

A custom domain name is the final step in creating a professional online presence. Instead of a generic hosting URL, you'll have a memorable domain like yourname.com that builds credibility and makes your portfolio easy to find and share.

Why a Custom Domain Matters

Your domain name is your digital identity. It affects how potential clients, employers, and collaborators perceive your work and remember your portfolio.

Professional Benefits

  • Credibility: yourname.com looks more professional than random-site-123.netlify.app
  • Memorability: Easy for people to remember and revisit
  • SEO: Better search engine rankings with relevant domain names
  • Email: Create professional email addresses like hello@yourname.com
  • Control: You own the domain regardless of hosting provider
  • Branding: Consistent identity across all platforms

Choosing Your Domain Name

Your domain name should be simple, memorable, and represent your brand:

Domain Strategy Options

Strategy Example Best For Pros
Personal Name johndoe.com Personal branding Clear identity, flexible content
Name + Portfolio johndoeportfolio.com Portfolio sites Clear purpose, available names
Name + Art johndoeart.com Artists Niche focus, memorable
Studio Name pixelstudios.com Business/agency Scalable, brandable

Domain Name Guidelines

• Keep it short and easy to spell
• Avoid hyphens and numbers
• Use .com if available (most trusted)
• Check social media availability
• Consider future expansion

Connecting Domain to Netlify

Step 1: Add Domain in Netlify

  1. Go to your Netlify site dashboard
  2. Click "Site settings" → "Domain management"
  3. Click "Add custom domain"
  4. Enter your domain (e.g., yourname.com)
  5. Click "Verify" and then "Add domain"
  6. Netlify will provide DNS settings

Step 2: Configure DNS in Namecheap

In your Namecheap dashboard:

  1. Go to "Domain List" and click "Manage" next to your domain
  2. Go to the "Advanced DNS" tab
  3. Delete any existing A Records and CNAME Records
  4. Add the DNS records provided by Netlify

Required DNS Records

# A Records (for apex domain) Type: A Record Host: @ Value: 75.2.60.5 TTL: Automatic # CNAME Record (for www subdomain) Type: CNAME Record Host: www Value: your-site-name.netlify.app TTL: Automatic # Example if your Netlify site is "john-doe-portfolio.netlify.app": # CNAME: www → john-doe-portfolio.netlify.app

Step 3: Wait for Propagation

DNS changes can take 24-48 hours to fully propagate worldwide. You can check the status using online DNS checker tools.

Step 4: Enable HTTPS

Once your domain is connected, Netlify will automatically provision an SSL certificate. This usually takes a few minutes to complete.
